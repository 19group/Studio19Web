Client: Mjasiri Platform

Event: Mjasiri: Dear Future Generation Event - a theme which celebrated the successes of Women, Environment and people who are defying barriers.

Venue: The Little Theatre, Dar es Salaam, Tanzania

Project Details: A Studio 19 initiative, this two-hour interactive show titled “Dear Future Generation” took place at the Little Theatre in Dar es Salaam, engaged a live audience of 100+ and included 6 speakers including renowned writer and participatory trainer, Richard Mabala, and seasoned academician and researcher Dr Elifuraha Mtalo. Mjasiri also included dramatic performances from Dr. Vicensia Shule and music from the talented Maembe.

Studio 19’s role: Conceptualization, Design and branding, Programme preparation, briefing presenters, logistics, and attendee registration.
