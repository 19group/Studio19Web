FOSS4G 2018 - Dar es salaam, Tanzania

OSGEO/ Free and Open Source Software for Geospatial (FOSS4G) Local Organizing Committee 2018

View All Images

Client: OSGEO/ Free and Open Source Software for Geospatial (FOSS4G) Local Organizing Committee 2018

Event: A one-week international conference (27 August to 2 September 2018)

Venue: Julius Nyerere International Conference Centre (JNICC), Dar es Salaam

Project Details: The conference brought together over 1,100 programmers, thinkers, designers and practitioners from around the world, and exhibits from Google, ESRI, Microsoft, the World Bank among many others. Through six days of workshops, presentations, and discussions, FOSS4G 2018 created an effective and welcoming environment for this global conference, setting a new standard in the process.

Studio 19’s role: Communication and registration of the 1000+ attendees, design and branding, accommodations and transportation for all attendees, handling photography and videography for the event’s multiple sessions across the six days and creating both a welcome video and a farewell video which were played on the first and last day, respectively. We also organized two gala nights and entertainment, and organized excursions for the spouses of attendees to enjoy the beauty that Tanzania has to offer.
