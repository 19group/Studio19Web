Our Clients.

Who Are We.

Studio 19 Limited was born in 2015 as a creative media company that is focused on telling Africa's stories, with the emphasis on using creative media and technology, to express what is inherent in the African continent.

Our Approach.

We approach every new project with an incredible passion and commitment to achieve the best out of the project. Our desire to produce great work enables us to tackle every project with enthusiasm and full of energy.

While we offer our expertise, we listen attentively to understand your problem, and make sure your expectations are met.

Our Process.

We offer one free consultation with all our clients, because the more we understand your business and your problems better, the more we can be of more help.

Thus feel free to contact us, and we will be happy to meet and talk to you.
